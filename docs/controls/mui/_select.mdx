#### *Options format

:::tip
See [controls options](./controls-options) for full options specs.
:::

- Options props are forwarded to [`<option />`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/option) or [`<MenuItem />`](https://mui.com/api/menu-item/) elements. (depending on `native` value.)
- Group props are forwarded to [`<optgroup />`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/optgroup) or [`<ListSubheader />`](https://mui.com/api/list-subheader/) elements. (depending on `native` value.)
